### **1. Audience Targeting**

- Start narrow with **specific job titles, industries, and seniority** rather than broad audiences.
- Use **Matched Audiences** (upload customer/prospect lists or retarget site visitors).
    
    → Warmer audiences tend to deliver higher engagement and conversion rates.
    
- Exclude irrelevant job functions, geographies, or company sizes.
    
    → Keeps your targeting clean and prevents wasted spend.
    

---

### **2. Bidding & Budget**

- While LinkedIn recommends bid ranges, **set your bid higher than suggested**.
    
    → Higher bids give you more visibility and improve delivery in competitive segments.
    
- Monitor **Cost per Result** (CPC, CPL) weekly.
    
    → Prevents runaway spend and allows you to reallocate to top performers.
    

---

### **3. Ad Creative & Copy**

- Keep headlines **short and direct** (under 150 characters).
    
    → Prospects skim quickly; clarity beats cleverness.
    
- Use **single, clear CTAs** like *“Book a Demo”* or *“Download the Guide.”*
    
    → Reduces confusion and increases click-through rates.
    
- Test multiple formats (Single Image, Carousel, Video).
    
    → Different formats appeal to different personas and stages of the funnel.
    

---

### **4. Campaign Structure**

- Group ads logically by **audience or objective**.
    
    → Makes reporting cleaner and easier to optimize.
    

---

### **5. Content Strategy**

- Refresh creatives every **4–6 weeks**.
    
    → Prevents ad fatigue and declining CTR.
    
- Layer **thought leadership posts** with paid campaigns.
    
    → Builds credibility and improves paid ad performance.
    

---

### **6. Retargeting & Sequencing**

- Retarget based on **engagement stage** (site visitors, lead form openers, content viewers).
    
    → Warmer audiences typically convert at 2–3x the rate of cold outreach.
    
- Use **message sequencing**:
    1. Awareness (content or thought leadership)
    2. Consideration (case studies, guides)
    3. Conversion (demo offers, free trial)
- This builds trust instead of jumping straight to a hard sell.

---

✅ **Quick Recap:**

- Target smartly → Avoid wasted impressions.
- Bid aggressively → Secure visibility.
- Test often → Find what works.
- Track conversions → Prove ROI.

# **Setting Up an A/B Test Parameters**

---

### **Step 1: Select Your Campaign**

- In [LinkedIn Campaign Manager](https://www.linkedin.com/campaignmanager?utm_source=chatgpt.com), log in and choose the correct **ad account**
- From the left-hand navigation, click **Campaigns**.
- From the left-hand navigation, select **Advertise → Campaigns**
- Click on the title the **campaign** where you want to run the A/B test within.
- That brings you to the **Ads tab** where you will be able to create a duplicative ad — your page vs. Architects

---

### **Step 2: Duplicate Your Existing Ad**

- In the **Ads tab**, locate the ad you want to test (the “Control” ad with your Company’s landing page you want to test).
- Click the checkbox next to the ad name.
- Click **Duplicate** at the top of the screen.
- This creates a copy of the ad, keeping the same targeting, creative, and settings.

---

### **Step 3: Edit the Test Ad (Architect Landing Page Version)**

- Open the duplicated ad for editing.
- Update the **Destination URL** to the **Architect landing page URL**.
- Keep all other elements the same (headline, copy, creative, CTA).
    - This ensures the **only variable being tested is the landing page.**

---

### **Step 4: Analyze Results**

- Run the test for a set period (at least 2–4 weeks for statistical relevance).
- Compare metrics between the original landing page and the Architect landing page.
- Use these insights to test Architect vs. internal landing page results.

---

✅ **Additional Tips:**

- Keep all variables except the landing page identical (copy, targeting, audience, bid price). This isolates the landing page as the **only difference** and ensures the test is valid.
- Don’t pause one of the ads during the test, allow both to run in parallel.

**Adding a goal / call‑to‑action (CTA) to your page**

- **Step 1: Open your page group**

Sign in to Architect, go to **Pages**, and click the page group you want to edit, in the top left corner.

- **Step 2:  Open the page group settings**
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-12-15 at 10.44.09.png](/documentation/images/Screenshot_2025-12-15_at_10.44.09.png)
    

In the page group editor, look for the sidebar for that page group.

- **Step 3: Go to Branding and Actions**
- **Step 4 : Set up the CTA**

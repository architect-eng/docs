title: "What is Architect?"
description: "An overview of Architect and what you can use it for"
---

Architect is an agentic website that rebuilds your site around each visitor in real time. It uses your approved knowledge and assets to generate a page that matches who the visitor is, what they care about, and where they came from.

## What makes it different

- Your website is not static; it adapts to each visitor.
- You maintain one source of truth (your knowledge and assets).
- You can see what converts and iterate faster.

## Different use cases

- ABM: build personalized pages for target accounts.
- Ads: create message-matched pages for paid traffic.
- Follow ups: share a tailored page after a sales call.

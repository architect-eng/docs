### What to add to knowledge hub

- **Company Overview:**  Give as much information about the company such as key offerings, key pillars, pricing, statistics, products, e.t.c.
    - Examples include:
        - Product one-pagers
        - Sales memos
        - Case studies or customer one-pagers
        - Testimonials or customer quotes
        - Awards, certifications, or third-party recognitions
        - Setup / implementation guides
- **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):** Addressing key user inquiries and clarifying product features.
- **Competitive Analysis:** Focused comparisons for user education and marketing content.
- **Marketing Content & Context**
    - Examples include:
        - Campaign assets
        - Blog posts
        - Ad creatives

### How to Add Knowledge

1. On the left side bar in the Architect Platform go to Knowledge 
2. Add all the relevant information

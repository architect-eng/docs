## Making your first page

1. Upload images.
2. Upload information about yourself as a company.
3. Create a new Page Group and give it a page context.
4. Look at the page as it is being made.
5. Click publish and copy the link.

Voila, your first page is made.

See [Making your first page](/documentation/getting-started/making-your-first-page).

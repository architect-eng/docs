## Making your first page

Sign in at [app.tryarchitect.com](https://app.tryarchitect.com).

1. Upload images.
2. Upload information about yourself as a company.
3. Create a new Page Group and give it a page context.
4. Look at the page as it is being made.
5. Click publish and copy the link.

## How to do each step

### Upload images

Go to **Page Setup > Assets**, then upload images.

### Upload information about your company

Go to **Page Setup > Company Knowledge**, then add your documentation and context.

### Create a Page Group and add page context

Go to **Page Groups > + New Page Group** and fill in the purpose and context.

### Watch the page build

Open the page and watch it generate.

### Publish and copy the link

Open the page, click **Publish**, then copy the page link.

Voila, your first page is made.

See [Making your first page](/documentation/getting-started/making-your-first-page).

**1. Hero Images (2+)**

- Use soft or subtly textured backgrounds that allow text overlays to remain legible.
- Choose images that convey the overall tone or emotion of the product, service, or message.
- Avoid overly busy visuals or high-contrast patterns that distract from key headlines.

**2. Product or Core Offering Visuals (5+)**

- Include clear, high-resolution visuals of the product, app interface, or main service.
- Focus on visuals that communicate value or demonstrate use cases.
- Ensure consistency in lighting, perspective, and colour treatment for a cohesive look.

**3. Supporting Visuals (3+)**

- Add graphics, illustrations, or comparison visuals that help explain key concepts or features.
- Examples: side-by-side comparisons, process diagrams, or benefit breakdowns.

1. **Logos/Awards** 
- Any Logo’s of existing customers , suppliers  or awards.
- In your Image Description, make sure to specify who’s logo this is and wether its a customer, supplier or anything else.

## Where to Upload Images?

2. On the left side navigation bar, go to “Assets”
3. Click “Upload Images”

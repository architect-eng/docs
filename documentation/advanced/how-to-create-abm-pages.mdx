# ⭐ What Are ABM Pages?

ABM Pages are **personalized microsites or landing pages** tailored to a specific target account (or a small set of accounts). They centralize content, messaging, and CTAs relevant to that account’s needs, industry, and buying stage.

---

# 🧱 How to Create ABM Pages (Step-by-Step)

## 1. **Define Your Target Accounts**

Before you build anything:

- Identify your high-value accounts
- Confirm the buying committee (roles/personas)
- Clarify where the account is in the funnel (awareness, consideration, procurement, expansion)

This helps determine what content and personalization are needed.

---

## 2. **Research the Account**

Gather insights about the company such as:

- Industry trends
- Current challenges/pain points
- Their tech stack
- Recent news, funding, or initiatives
- Key decision-makers and their motivations
- Your historical relationship (calls, POCs, emails, etc.)

This information will shape your page content and personalization.

---

## 3. **Decide What Goes on the Page**

Typical ABM Pages include:

### 🔹 Personalized Hero Section

- Logo of the target company
- Tailored headline (“How **Company X** Can Increase Product Velocity by 30%")

### 🔹 Curated Content

- Product overview (short and relevant)
- Case studies from similar industries or use cases
- ROI calculators

### 🔹 Social Proof

- Testimonials
- Awards
- Relevant customer logos
- Data points

### 🔹 Call-To-Action

Examples:

- “Schedule a Strategy Call”
- “Get Your Custom ROI Assessment”
- “Start a Technical Trial”

## **5. Connect to your CRM (Optional)**

Once your CRM is connected, you can automate the creation of ABM Pages at scale using our API and Webhooks. This turns your CRM into the engine that drives fully personalizsd, always-up-to-date pages — without manual work.

### 🔹 Use CRM Fields to Auto-Generate Pages

Define rules that automatically create a new ABM Page when:

- An account reaches a specific tier (e.g., Tier 1 or ICP)
- A deal enters a target stage (e.g., “Qualification” or “Value Prop”)
- A new high-value account is added to a target list
- A key persona joins the account

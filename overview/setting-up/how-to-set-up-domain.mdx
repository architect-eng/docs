## Set up and verify your domain

1. In the Architect platform, open the **Domains** tab.
2. Click **+ Add Domain**.
3. Enter the domain you want to publish pages on.

**Note:** do not include “www” before your domain.

![Screenshot 2025-10-27 at 17.43.17.png](/overview/images/Screenshot_2025-10-27_at_17.43.17.png)

4. Add the DNS records shown in the product (in your DNS provider).
5. Back in Architect, click **Check Now**.

<Check>
When the DNS records are correct, the domain status changes to **Verified**.
</Check>

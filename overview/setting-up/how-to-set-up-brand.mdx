---
title: "Set up your brand"
description: "Add your logo, colors, and typography so pages publish on-brand."
---

Your brand controls how Architect styles generated pages.

At a minimum, set up:

- Logos
- Primary color
- Typography

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a brand">
    Go to **Page Setup > Brands** and create a new brand.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your logos">
    Upload your primary logo (and a mark/icon if you have one).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your colors">
    Set a primary brand color (used for buttons, links, and emphasis).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set typography">
    Choose fonts for headings and body text.

    <Tip>
    Keep it simple: one display font for headings and one highly readable font for body.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify on a generated page">
    Generate or open a page and confirm it matches your brand.

    <Check>
    Buttons, headings, and section spacing look consistent and on-brand.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

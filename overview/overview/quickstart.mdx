## Making your first page

Sign in at [app.tryarchitect.com](https://app.tryarchitect.com).

1. Upload images.
2. Upload information about yourself as a company.
3. Create your brand.
4. Create a new Page Group and give it a page context.
5. Look at the page as it is being made.
6. Click publish and copy the link.

## How to do each step

### Upload images

Go to **Page Setup > Assets**, then upload images.

<Tip>
Add a few hero images, product visuals, and logos. The better your asset library, the better pages can stay on-brand.
</Tip>

### Upload information about your company

Go to **Page Setup > Company Knowledge**, then add your documentation and context.

<Tip>
Start with a short company overview, your core offerings, proof points, and a few FAQs. You can add more over time.
</Tip>

### Create your brand

Go to **Page Setup > Brands** and create a brand.

This brand is applied to pages in your Page Groups so your generated pages look consistent.

### Create a Page Group and add page context

Go to **Page Groups > + New Page Group** and fill in the purpose and context.

Then create a new page in the Page Group and give it context such as: "create a page in spanish around our product, educate the user and push to CTA".

<Tip>
If you are not sure which purpose to choose, start with the traffic source (Paid Ads vs. SEO/GEO) or the motion (ABM vs. Post Sales Call Follow Up).
</Tip>

### Watch the page build

Open the page and watch it generate.

### Publish and copy the link

Open the page, click **Publish**, then copy the page link.

Voila, your first page is made.

<Check>
Open the published link in an incognito window to verify it loads, looks on-brand, and the CTA goes to the right destination.
</Check>

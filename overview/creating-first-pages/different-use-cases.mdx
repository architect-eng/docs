Architect is built for teams that want one website system that can flex across many go-to-market motions.

## Educational material

Use Architect when your goal is to help a visitor understand something: what you do, how it works, and whether it is a fit. This is useful for inbound visitors who arrive with different levels of awareness and different questions.

Common outcomes:

- Clear positioning and proof without a wall of text
- Dynamic explanations that match the visitor’s context
- A CTA that guides the visitor to the right next step

## Ads

Use Architect for paid campaigns when message match matters. Instead of sending every click to the same static landing page, you can create Page Groups for specific campaigns and let the content adapt per visitor while keeping the CTA consistent.

Common outcomes:

- Better alignment between ad promise and landing experience
- Faster iteration without rebuilding landing pages
- Cleaner testing (Architect page vs. existing page)

## ABM

Use Architect for account-based motions when you want the page to feel tailored to a target account or persona. You can create personalized pages at different levels of granularity (by company, by role, or by individual).

Common outcomes:

- Higher relevance for target accounts
- A focused story that matches industry and buying stage
- A single link sales can send that stays on-message

## Post-sales call follow up

Use Architect to follow up after a meeting. The page becomes a recap plus a decision aid: it reinforces what mattered, shares the right proof, and keeps the buyer moving.

Common outcomes:

- A consistent follow-up that feels personal
- Fewer back-and-forth emails for links and assets
- A clear next step (book, trial, evaluation plan)

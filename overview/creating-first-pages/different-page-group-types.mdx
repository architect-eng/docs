## Picking a type

When you create a Page Group, set a **Page Group Type**:

ABM, SEO/GEO, Paid Ads, Post Sales Call Follow Up, or Custom.

A Page Group Type is a simple way to tell Architect what you are trying to achieve, so it can shape the page structure, content, and CTA around that goal. The type lives at the Page Group level, which means it applies to every page generated inside that group.

## When to use each type

Use these as defaults. You can always choose Custom when your goal does not fit cleanly.

### ABM

Choose **ABM** when you are targeting specific accounts (or roles inside accounts) and you want the page to feel like it was built for that buyer. This is best when you have account context (company, industry, buying stage, prior interactions) and you want a clear CTA like booking a call, starting a trial, or requesting a custom assessment.

### SEO/GEO

Choose **SEO/GEO** when you want pages that can work for inbound discovery. The goal is to communicate a topic clearly, build trust quickly, and guide a visitor to the right next step. This type is useful when the same core topic needs to adapt to different intents (for example, different industries or levels of awareness) without creating separate static pages for each.

### Paid Ads

Choose **Paid Ads** when the visitor is coming from a specific campaign and message match matters. The page should reflect the promise of the ad, stay focused, and drive the next action with minimal friction.

### Post Sales Call Follow Up

Choose **Post Sales Call Follow Up** when you want a page that continues the conversation after a meeting. This is ideal for recapping what mattered, sharing the most relevant proof points, and moving the deal forward with a single clear CTA.

### Custom

Choose **Custom** when you have a unique motion, a non-standard CTA, or special constraints. Use the Page Group Context to describe what success looks like, what the page must include, and what it should avoid.

<Tip>
If you are unsure, start with the type that matches your traffic source (Paid Ads vs. SEO/GEO) or your motion (ABM vs. Post Sales Call Follow Up). You can change the type later without rebuilding your knowledge base.
</Tip>

## Page groups and page context

A Page Group is the shared container for a set of pages.

In the Page Group settings, you define:

- **Page Group Type**: ABM, SEO/GEO, Paid Ads, Post Sales Call Follow Up, or Custom.
- **Page Group Context**: the shared objective, audience, and constraints used for generation.
- **Brand**: the brand styling applied to pages in the group.

Each individual page can also have its own optional page context.

The page context is appended to the page group context and sent to the agent.

## Slices (building blocks)

Pages are built from non-static components.

On the left-hand side, you have building blocks like widgets and the Action Card (your final destination).

We call everything on the page slices.

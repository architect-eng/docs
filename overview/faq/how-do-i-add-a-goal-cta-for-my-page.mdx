**Adding a goal / call‑to‑action (CTA) to your page**

- **Step 1: Open your page group**

Sign in to Architect, go to **Pages**, and click the page group you want to edit, in the top left corner.

- **Step 2:  Open the page group settings**
    
    ![Screenshot 2025-12-15 at 10.44.09.png](/overview/images/Screenshot_2025-12-15_at_10.44.09.png)
    

In the page group editor, look for the sidebar for that page group.

- **Step 3: Go to Branding and Actions**
- **Step 4 : Set up the CTA**

In the Page Group settings, open **Branding and Actions**.

Set the CTA to match what you want the visitor to do next. Typically this is one of:

- **Open URL**: send the visitor to a booking link, signup page, or product destination
- **Form**: capture lead details directly on the page

<Tip>
Keep the CTA specific and action-based (for example, “Book a demo” or “Get pricing”). If you are running ads, keep the CTA consistent across the group so performance is easier to compare.
</Tip>

<Check>
After saving, open a generated page and click the CTA to verify it goes to the right destination.
</Check>

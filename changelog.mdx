<Update label="April 13th, 2026" description="Better analytics, smarter notifications, bulk management, refreshed UI, richer assets">

    ## Notifications & Lead Alerts

    Stay informed when new leads arrive and make sure the right people see the right updates.

    - **Form Submission Alerts:** We added a new notifications service and email templates for form submissions, so your team can respond to high-intent leads quickly.
    - **Digest Notifications:** Notification digests now bundle important activity into recaps, making it easier to catch up on lead and account activity without checking constantly.
    - **Notification Preferences:** A new notification preferences UI lets each user control what they want to receive, reducing noise while keeping sales and marketing teams in the loop.

    ## Bulk Actions & Faster Page Management

    We’ve made it much easier to manage pages, audiences, and prospects in bulk.

    - **Bulk Prospect Actions:** You can now export prospects to CSV, import prospects from CSV, and create pages for multiple prospects at once.
    - **Bulk Page Creation:** Create multiple pages from a prospect list with supporting CSV and page-link workflows, saving time on ABM campaigns and outreach lists.
    - **Account-Level Data:** Prospects and activity are now grouped with account context, helping you see which companies are engaging with your pages — ideal for ABM and sales follow-up.
    - **Bulk Ownership Tools:** The prospects page now includes a more polished bulk assign owners flow, so sales teams can quickly route leads to the right person.
    - **Cleaner Tables and Filters:** We added owner filtering, removed noisy columns, and improved grouping so you can find the right prospects and accounts faster.

    ![Screenshot of bulk actions / prospects management UI](/changelog/images/prospects-2026-04-13.png)

    ## Management UI & Terminology Updates

    The management experience has been streamlined to feel clearer, simpler, and more consistent for non-technical users.

    - **Pages and Audiences Renaming:** We’ve updated terminology across the management UI to better match how customers think about their content and targets.
    - **Smoother Settings Experience:** Settings have been reorganised into clearer sections and sub-tabs, making it easier to manage SEO, brand, actions, scripts, and form settings.
    - **Better Page Creation Flow:** The create audience and page flows now have improved layouts, inline feedback, and less confusing navigation.
    - **Polished Buttons and Modals:** We normalised button labels, modal styling, and dialog behaviour for a more consistent and professional interface.
    - **Brain Rebrand:** The old knowledge area has been rebuilt as Brain, making the learning and guidance area feel more focused and easier to navigate.

    ![Screenshot of the new Brain page](/changelog/images/knowledge-2026-04-13.png)
    ![Screenshot of the updated management UI](/changelog/images/overview-2026-04-13.png)

    ## Brain & Prompting Improvements

    We’ve also improved the underlying “brain” so generated experiences are more useful and less error-prone.

    - **Better Conversation Reliability:** Chat, inline conversation, and reload behaviour are more stable, which helps generated pages feel smoother for visitors.
    - **Improved Keyword Cards:** Keyword metadata now powers richer hover cards and click interactions, giving visitors more context without cluttering the page.
    - **Safer Generation Logic:** We tightened prompt handling and content validation so page generation is more consistent and less likely to break edge cases.
    - **Faster, More Reliable Page Rendering:** We’ve improved loading states, skeletons, and version handling to reduce awkward delays when pages are being created or updated.

    ## Assets & Visual Content

    Your pages can now use richer media with less friction.

    - **URL-Based Asset Imports:** Paste a URL and Architect will now detect whether it’s an image or video and import it for you.
    - **Improved Asset Review Flow:** Uploads now include a review step so you can rename files and confirm asset types before publishing.
    - **Better Image Handling:** We restored image import flows and improved asset selection, helping you build more polished landing pages faster.
    - **New Visual Components:** Several UI and content components have been refined so your pages look cleaner and more branded on mobile and desktop.

    ![Screenshot of the asset library import flow](/changelog/images/assets-2026-04-13.png)

</Update>


<Update label="April 1st, 2026" description="Interactive Pages, Prospect Management, and Global Workspace Controls">

    ## Interactive On-Page AI Experiences

    We've supercharged your on-page AI to help you automatically engage visitors, answer their objections, and drive them to book a call through dynamic page elements.

    - **Page Agent Chat:** Visitors can now interact with our Page Agent through the 'Ask me anything' bar, allowing them to ask questions and generate personalized page sections.
    - **Inline Conversations:** Hover over any element on the page and see personalized suggestions to expand the conversation. Instead of having a one-size-fits-all FAQ, we let the agent predict the most frequently asked questions for every component on your page: text paragraphs, features cards, images, etc. *(Note: Available on newly generated pages only.)*
    - **Keyword Cards:** Highlighted text keywords are now interactive! Click on one and the agent will pull out a definition or explanation card. *(Note: Available on newly generated pages only. You might also notice that the highlighted words on your page have changed colors. If desired, you can always change that color back by heading to your Brand Settings under 'Links/ Highlights' color.)*

    ![Keyword highlights on-page AI screenshot](/changelog/images/keyword-highlights-2026-04-01.png)

    ## Enhanced People Tracking & Management

    Managing the people you are targeting for your Outbound campaigns just got a lot easier and more flexible.

    - **Redesigned Prospects Dashboard:** Easily search, sort, and group your leads, and view their activity and personalized pages side-by-side.
    - **More Ways to Capture Details:** You can now save LinkedIn profiles, company domains, and automatically-formatted international phone numbers directly to your prospect records.
    - 🔜 Coming Soon: Bulk Upload, Export and Page Creation for Prospects

    ## Global Workspace & AI Knowledge Settings

    We've added new centralized controls and navigation tools to help you manage your brand's knowledge base and move through the platform with less friction.

    - **Global Search (Cmd+K):** Jump instantly to any campaign, page, or prospect from anywhere in the app using our new global search shortcut.
    - **Global AI Rules:** Take full control of your brand voice. Set workspace-wide "rules" for your AI—tell it exactly how to speak, what value props to emphasize, and how to represent your business across all your pages.
    - **Document-Grounded Answers:** You can now upload your company documents and the AI will use them to provide smarter, more specific answers to your visitors' questions.

    Find Documents and AI Rules [**here**](https://app.tryarchitect.com/page-setup/knowledge).

<Update label="March 10th, 2026" description="Page Cloning, Better Analytics, and New Components">

    ## Functionality Improvements
    
    Streamline your workflow, test campaigns faster, and track your data with pinpoint accuracy.
    
    - **Page Cloning:** Duplicate any page right from your dashboard. Easily reuse your highest-converting layouts across different Page Groups or set up quick A/B tests. Just clone the page, tell the agent what to tweak, and hit publish.
    - **Page Group Types:** When creating or editing a Page Group, you can now select a purpose type. This tells our AI exactly what kind of page to build, automatically tailoring the structure, tone, and messaging to match your goal.
    - **Stop the AI Mid-Build:** You can now hit stop mid-generation. It safely saves the draft right where it stopped, allowing you to instantly tweak the design on the fly.
    - **Full UTM Tracking on Forms:** When a visitor fills out a form, full UTM parameters are now captured and passed directly through your webhook into your CRM. You'll know exactly which ad, email, or campaign drove the conversion.
  
    ## Page Improvements & New Components
    
    Deliver faster, fully compliant, and seamless experiences to your page visitors.
    
    - **Cookie Banner:** Architect now has its own custom cookie banner. Fully customizable to match your brand's styling, it automatically targets visitors based on their region to ensure compliance. It also works seamlessly with Google Consent Mode v2 to keep your Google Ads and third-party analytics strictly compliant, all while your native Architect conversion metrics continue to track perfectly.

    - **Page Image Loading:** We've upgraded how images load on your pages using progressive placeholders and responsive sizing. This gives your visitors a much smoother mobile experience and boosts your page speed (a big win for SEO).
</Update>

<Update label="March 2nd, 2026" description="Analytics Dashboards, Routing and Smarter Asset Management">

    ## Enhanced Asset Library

    We have completely rebuilt the way you manage images and files to speed up your design workflow, separating assets into different categories.

    - **Delete Images:** Keep your workspace tidy by deleting old images, logos, or icons you no longer need.
    - **Instant Logo Imports:** You can now search for a brand domain and automatically import their official logos directly into your library.
    - **Image Scraping:** Enter your website URL to instantly pull and bulk-upload existing images from your website.

    ![Asset library image](/changelog/images/image-18.png)

    ## Analytics & Reporting

    Gain deeper visibility into how your landing pages are performing across your campaigns.

    - **Analytics Dashboard:** View high-level metrics for all your page groups and pages in one place. Easily track session counts and engagement over time to see which pages are winning.
    - **Excel & CSV Export:** You can now export your analytics data directly to Excel or CSV. Perfect for sharing campaign results with stakeholders or combining data with your own internal reports.

    ## Routing and Experiments

    Take full control of your traffic distribution and run smarter tests with our new routing and experimentation capabilities.

    - **Weighted Traffic Routing:** Distribute traffic across multiple pages using customizable weights. Perfect for A/B testing, gradual rollouts, or splitting traffic between different pages.
    - **External URL Experiments:** Compare your existing landing pages against ours using a single, unified link, where you decide how to split the traffic. This is specifically useful if you are running PMAX Campaigns, as you are unable to run these experiments in Google.
    - **Query Parameter Preservation:** UTM parameters and tracking data automatically flow through redirects, ensuring your attribution stays intact across the entire visitor journey.
</Update>

    We're expanding your design toolkit with highly requested building blocks to make your pages more engaging and conversion-friendly.

    - **Video Component:** Video is one of the highest-converting assets for landing pages. We've added a dedicated workflow to manage and embed YouTube videos onto your page.

    - **Web Search:** The agent can now browse the web to gather context on your company or competitors, resulting in much more relevant copy and design.

<Update label="February 5th, 2026" description="V2 Agent, Native Analytics, & Global Knowledge">

    ## New Agent Experience: Build & Edit in Real-Time

    We are introducing a new AI Chat Agent interface to speed up your page creation workflow.

    - **Chat-to-Build:** Simply chat with the agent to generate or modify landing pages. This allows for rapid iteration when spinning up personalized pages for different target accounts.
    - **Live Streaming:** Watch your page build itself in real-time. As the agent generates content, the preview updates instantly, no more waiting for the whole page to finish processing.
    - **Direct Slice Editing:** You can now ask the agent to edit specific sections (slices) of a page directly, making it easier to tweak copy or layout for specific customer segments without rebuilding the whole page.

    Metabase has been officially shut down and is no longer available. To streamline your workflow, we have moved all analytics directly into the platform.

    - **Full Migration to In-Platform:** You can now access all your campaign data without ever leaving your dashboard. All tracking, including UTM parameters and Date Range filtering, is now exclusively located in the Analytics tab within your page groups.
    - **Upcoming V2 Agent Metrics:** We are currently integrating deep-dive analytics for the new Agent. Soon, you'll see native tracking for threading, FAQ interactions, clicks, and scroll behavior right alongside your traffic data.

    ## Visual Polish: Highlighting & Animations

    First impressions matter. We've added sophisticated animations to keep visitors engaged and guide their eyes to your key value propositions.

    - **Enhanced Page Animations:** We've improved the motion design throughout the entire page experience. From smooth transitions to subtle entrance effects, the platform now feels more fluid and high-end.
    - **Tailored Branding:** Customize your look and feel with ease. From custom CSS to specialized font libraries, you can now direct the agent to style your pages to be pixel-perfect and on-brand.
    - **Coming Soon:** Video components, logo groupings, and icon sets.
</Update>

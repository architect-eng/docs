<Update label="March 10th, 2026" description="Page Cloning, Better Analytics, and New Components">
    We've shipped features to help you scale your ad campaigns faster, track your leads more accurately, and build beautifully engaging pages.
    
    ## Functionality Improvements 
    
    Streamline your workflow, test campaigns faster, and track your data with pinpoint accuracy.
    
    - **Page Cloning:** Duplicate any page right from your dashboard. Easily reuse your highest-converting layouts across different Page Groups or set up quick A/B tests. Just clone the page, tell the agent what to tweak, and hit publish.
    - **Page Group Types:** When creating or editing a Page Group, you can now select a purpose type. This tells our AI exactly what kind of page to build, automatically tailoring the structure, tone, and messaging to match your goal.
    - **Stop the AI Mid-Build:** You can now hit stop mid-generation. It safely saves the draft right where it stopped, allowing you to instantly tweak the design on the fly.
    - **Full UTM Tracking on Forms:** When a visitor fills out a form, full UTM parameters are now captured and passed directly through your webhook into your CRM. You'll know exactly which ad, email, or campaign drove the conversion. 
  
    ## Page Improvements & New Components 
    
    Deliver faster, fully compliant, and seamless experiences to your page visitors.
    
    - **Cookie Banner:** Architect now has its own custom cookie banner. Fully customizable to match your brand's styling, it automatically targets visitors based on their region to ensure compliance. It also works seamlessly with Google Consent Mode v2 to keep your Google Ads and third-party analytics strictly compliant, all while your native Architect conversion metrics continue to track perfectly.

    - **Page Image Loading:** We've upgraded how images load on your pages using progressive placeholders and responsive sizing. This gives your visitors a much smoother mobile experience and boosts your page speed (a big win for SEO).
</Update>
